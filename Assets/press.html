Gold for Canada!

December 2014

Caitlin returned from Mikolajki, Poland, from the International Dance Organization World Dance Championships in December 2014, where she supported Team Canada West as head ballet choreographer. Her choreography Rojo, Dollhouse, Pas de Sept, and Peasant March each won gold medals in their categories, while Pompeii took home a silver and Tarantella placed sixth overall. Thank you Team Canada West dancers for your dedication and hard work!

BC Children's Hospital A Night of Miracles Fundraiser

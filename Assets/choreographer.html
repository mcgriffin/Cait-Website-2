Choreography

When I create a dance, my idea is to begin with a sketch of movement and images, and then to decide what those can mean. Then I try and refine that sketch into a framework that leaves room for inspiration and reaction on stage by the performers in real time. It’s a combination of structure and freedom that I hope gives the performers and audience opportunities to recognize one another’s experience in the space.
